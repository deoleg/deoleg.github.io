Partner With Us

We believe that God invites many people to be part of His work — each in different ways. Some go. Many support. All can pray.

Pray

Prayer is foundational to everything we do.

We ask our partners to pray for spiritual openness among people we serve, growth and maturity of new believers, and wisdom, faithfulness, and balance for our family.

We are deeply grateful for those who walk with us in prayer.

Financial support makes our ministry possible and allows us to serve full-time.

Giving is not about obligation, but about shared vision and trust in God’s work.
