Help Us Build What's Next

We are part of a Cru team working to strengthen and grow the church in Moldova — specifically among young people. Every year, our team plans projects that create real moments of change in students' lives.

Here is what your gift makes possible:

English Week — Once or twice a year, we bring students together for a full week of English learning, community, and Gospel conversations. For many of them, it is the first time they hear about Christ in a meaningful way.

Extreme Camp — Every summer, we take students into nature for a week of adventure, discipleship, and life-changing experiences. It is one of the highlights of the year for everyone involved.

Ongoing discipleship — After the big events, the real work begins. We walk with students week by week, helping them grow in their faith and become leaders in their churches.

To make all of this happen, our team needs $1,700 per month. Every dollar goes directly toward these projects and the students we serve.

God Answered This Need

A Reliable Ministry Vehicle

Some time ago, our family faced a practical need: the car we used for ministry was becoming unreliable and unsafe. Because our ministry requires frequent travel across Moldova, we began praying for a way to replace it.

We shared this need with our ministry partners and trusted God to provide. What happened next reminded us again how faithfully the Lord cares for His servants through His people.

Through the generosity of our partners in ministry, the full amount of $15,000 was given. We are deeply grateful for the way God answered this prayer and for every person who participated in this need.

This vehicle will help our family continue traveling across Moldova, serving churches, transporting worship equipment, and sharing the Gospel.

To God be the glory.
