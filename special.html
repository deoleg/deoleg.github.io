Special Needs Ministry

We’re committed to building a church where every individual — including those with special needs — is welcomed, valued, and empowered to grow in faith.

In Moldova, families caring for loved ones with disabilities often face isolation and a lack of spiritual support. We want to walk alongside them — offering community, practical help, and discipleship rooted in Jesus’ love.

Though each story is unique, our heart is simple: create spaces in the Church where every person belongs, every parent feels supported, and every child is encouraged to thrive.

Here we explore how local churches can be equipped to serve with dignity, compassion, and grace.
