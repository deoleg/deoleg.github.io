God Answered This Need

A Reliable Ministry Vehicle

Some time ago, our family faced a practical need: the car we used for ministry was becoming unreliable and unsafe. Because our ministry requires frequent travel across Moldova, we began praying for a way to replace it.

We shared this need with our ministry partners and trusted God to provide. What happened next reminded us again how faithfully the Lord cares for His servants through His people.

Through the generosity of our partners in ministry, the full amount of $15,000 was given. We are deeply grateful for the way God answered this prayer and for every person who participated in this need.

This vehicle will help our family continue traveling across Moldova, serving churches, transporting worship equipment, and sharing the Gospel.

To God be the glory.
