Special Needs Ministry

We believe the Church should be a place where every person is seen, valued, and loved — including families navigating special needs.

In Moldova, many families raising children with disabilities experience isolation, limited resources, and a lack of spiritual community. Our vision is to build a church culture where these families are welcomed, supported, and discipled.

Through intentional relationships, leadership development, and practical care, we want to equip local churches to serve individuals with special needs and their families with compassion and excellence.
