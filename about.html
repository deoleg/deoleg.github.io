About Us

We are Oleg and Karina Dermenji. We serve with Cru in Moldova, helping people encounter Jesus and grow as His disciples within local communities.

We were both born and raised in Moldova, in families where faith was not always central. Our journeys to Christ were personal and intentional, shaped by questions, struggle, and a desire to live consistently with what we believe.

During our student years, we became actively involved in local churches and ministry. It was there that we encountered missionaries serving with Cru — people who shared the Gospel relationally and helped others grow in their faith.

Over time, this vision became our own calling. In 2012, after a season of prayer and discernment, we joined Cru staff in Moldova and have continued serving ever since.
