About Us

We are Oleg and Karina Dermenji, serving with Cru in Moldova. Both of us grew up in non-Christian families and came to faith through relationships and local churches.

Music and ministry were an important part of our early journey. Over time, God shaped our calling toward relational evangelism and discipleship among young people.

In 2012, after a season of prayer and discernment, we joined Cru staff and have been serving together ever since.
