Ministry in Moldova

Moldova is one of the least reached countries in Europe. Many people identify culturally as Christian, yet few have a personal relationship with Jesus or are part of a healthy local church.

Decades of Soviet atheism, economic hardship, and large-scale migration have deeply shaped society. While churches exist, discipleship and leadership development remain limited in many regions.

Cru began serving in Moldova in 1995. Today, Cru Moldova is part of a global movement serving in nearly 200 countries, with a shared vision to see spiritual movements everywhere.

Our focus

Our ministry centers on helping people move from curiosity to faith, and from faith to mature discipleship.

We focus on relational evangelism, one-on-one discipleship, small group communities, and leadership development. We believe that faith grows best in relationships, not programs.

How we serve

Youth and Young Adults
Walking alongside young people as they navigate life, identity, and faith — helping them encounter Jesus, ask honest questions, and grow as His disciples.

Discipleship and Small Groups
Investing in small faith communities where believers learn to study Scripture, pray together, and live out their faith in everyday life.

Leader Development
Identifying and equipping local leaders who can multiply disciples and serve within their churches and communities.

Church and Community Engagement
Working alongside local churches to strengthen sustainable, long-term ministry.

Our vision

We long to see simple, healthy faith communities planted throughout Moldova — so that every person would know someone who truly follows Jesus.

Our role is not to replace the local church, but to serve it, strengthen it, and walk alongside it.
