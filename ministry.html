Our Ministry

Our ministry is focused on building spiritual movements within local communities. We walk alongside young people in everyday life, helping them encounter Jesus and grow as His disciples.

Over the years, we have served in various areas of Cru, including student ministry, digital strategy, and operations. Currently, we serve with the Global Church Movement team.

The vision of Global Church Movement is to see at least one multiplying home church in every community of one thousand people — communities actively sharing the Gospel, discipling new believers, and raising leaders for future churches.

Youth & Young Adults

We invest in long-term relationships with young people outside formal programs, creating space for honest conversations about faith, life, and calling.

Discipleship & Mentoring

Through one-on-one mentoring and small groups, we help believers grow spiritually and learn how to share their faith with others.

Local Communities

We serve alongside local churches, helping to strengthen spiritual movements that are rooted in community life and multiplication.
